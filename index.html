About SAMWA

The South African Municipal Wellness Association was founded in 1999 and has been providing quality sports and recreation to the municipalities ever since. Located in South Africa, SAMWA’s NEC members are dedicated to providing sports and wellness activities, contributing positively to South African municipalities in various remarkable ways.

Our Mission

To run a consistent and sustainable wellness programme for municipal employees and Councillors with opportunities to participate in recreational and competitive activities

Our Vision

A healthier, happier, and more engaged municipal workforce across South Africa.
